# National City Bank — Customer Propensity Modeling

## Business Problem
Identify customers most likely to adopt a new auto credit line to improve targeting efficiency and marketing ROI.

## Approach
- Cleaned and prepared demographic, behavioral, and financial data
- Engineered features for modeling
- Built logistic regression and random forest models
- Evaluated performance using internal validation and external data

## Results
- Internal validation AUC: ~0.90
- External validation AUC: 0.82
- Ranked 1,000 customers
- Identified top 100 high-probability prospects

## Business Impact
Enabled targeted marketing campaigns and improved customer acquisition strategy.
