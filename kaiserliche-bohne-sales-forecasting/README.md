# Kaiserliche Bohne — Sales Forecasting & Demand Analysis

## Business Problem
Forecast demand for key products to optimize inventory planning and reduce waste while ensuring product availability.

## Approach
- Cleaned and structured POS transaction data
- Aggregated time-series data for key products (e.g., cappuccino)
- Performed trend and seasonality analysis
- Built forecasting models (Holt-Winters and time series methods)
- Evaluated model performance using RMSE and MAPE

## Results
- Identified strong seasonal patterns in product demand
- Improved forecast accuracy compared to baseline methods
- Generated forward-looking demand projections for planning

## Business Impact
Enabled data-driven inventory and staffing decisions, reducing stockouts and overproduction.
