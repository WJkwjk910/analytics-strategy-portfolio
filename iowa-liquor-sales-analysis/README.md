# Iowa Liquor Sales Analysis

## Business Problem  
Identify revenue drivers, pricing patterns, and geographic opportunities to improve liquor sales performance across Iowa.

## Dataset  
- State-regulated liquor sales transactions (2019–2023)  
- 12M+ records including store, product, pricing, and location data  

## Approach  
- Merged multi-year datasets into a unified analysis table  
- Cleaned missing values and removed pricing outliers  
- Aggregated sales across time, product categories, and geography  
- Visualized trends using R (ggplot2)

## Key Insights  
- Sales peak during summer and holiday seasons → opportunity for inventory planning  
- Vodka and whiskey dominate sales → focus on high-demand categories  
- Majority of purchases fall within $10–$25 price range → key pricing band  
- Urban centers (Des Moines, Cedar Rapids) drive highest revenue  

## Business Impact  
- Supports inventory optimization during peak demand periods  
- Enables targeted marketing based on product and location performance  
- Provides pricing strategy guidance to maximize volume and margin  

## Tools  
R, dplyr, ggplot2, data.table
