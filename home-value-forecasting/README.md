# Home Value Forecasting — Real Estate Price Analysis

## Business Problem
Identify markets with the most consistent and highest growth in home values to support real estate investment decisions.

## Approach
- Analyzed historical housing price data across multiple regions
- Built time-series models to evaluate trends and growth patterns
- Measured consistency of price appreciation over time
- Compared regions based on growth stability and return potential

## Results
- Identified top-performing regions with sustained price growth
- Highlighted markets with high volatility vs. stable appreciation
- Provided ranked list of investment-attractive locations

## Business Impact
Enabled data-driven real estate investment decisions by identifying markets with strong and consistent growth potential.
